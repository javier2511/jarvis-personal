Privacy Policy

Jarvis is a personal AI assistant developed exclusively for personal use.

Information Collected

Jarvis only accesses services explicitly authorized by the user. These services may include:

  • Google Calendar
  • Spotify
  • WHOOP
  • Weather APIs
  • Maps APIs

How Information is Used

Data is used only to provide personalized assistance. No personal information is sold, shared, rented or transferred to third parties.

Security

OAuth tokens are stored securely and are only used to access services authorized by the user.
